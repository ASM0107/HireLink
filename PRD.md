# Contract Staffing & Recruitment Agency Platform

## Business Idea Overview
The platform is a technology-enabled recruitment and contract staffing agency focused on IT hiring. The business connects companies with pre-screened candidates and earns revenue through permanent placements and contract staffing arrangements. The long-term vision is to evolve from a traditional recruitment agency into an AI-powered talent acquisition platform.

## Business Goals
* Help companies hire faster.
* Build a large database of IT professionals.
* Automate candidate screening and matching.
* Generate revenue through hiring fees and staffing margins.
* Scale into a national IT staffing brand.

## Target Customers
* Startups
* Mid-size IT companies
* Enterprises
* Consulting firms
* Product companies

## Revenue Model
1. **Permanent Hiring:** Charge 8%–20% of annual CTC for successful placements.
2. **Contract Staffing:** Charge clients monthly and retain a staffing margin.
3. **Recruitment Process Outsourcing** (Future).
4. **Premium Talent Search Services** (Future).
